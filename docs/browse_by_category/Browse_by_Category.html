Browse by Category

Understanding Category-Based Browsing

Browsing cohort study variables by category allows researchers to quickly locate measures that share a common theme, such as demographics, lifestyle, clinical outcomes, or biological markers.

Grouping variables into intuitive categories helps users understand the structure of the metadata repository, identify related variables, and compare similar measures across time points or subgroups.

Navigating Topics and Subtopics in the Sidebar

The sidebar menu provides a clear, collapsible structure that makes it easy to move through topics and subtopics without scrolling through long pages.

  • Top‑level topics appear as main headings in the sidebar.
  • Subtopics automatically nest beneath their parent folders.
  • You can expand or collapse sections to focus on what you need.
  • The sidebar highlights the current page so you always know where you are.
  • Breadcrumb‑style structure helps you track your position within the documentation.
