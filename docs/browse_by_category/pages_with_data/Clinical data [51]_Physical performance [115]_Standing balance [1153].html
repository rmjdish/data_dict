During the clinic (or home) visit at age 60-64 years study participants were asked by the attending nurse whether they would be willing to perform tests of standing balance. If willing and able (reasons for inability included health reasons and equipment not available) two tests of standing balance were performed, one with eyes open and one with eyes closed.
