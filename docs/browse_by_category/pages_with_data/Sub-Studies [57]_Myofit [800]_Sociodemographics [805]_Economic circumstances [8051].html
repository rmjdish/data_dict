This category contains data on the economic circumstances of the participant such as household income and benefit details, recorded by the 1946 birth cohort participants who took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study. Participants who were unable to do answer the questions during the 2023 Home visit were asked to when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
