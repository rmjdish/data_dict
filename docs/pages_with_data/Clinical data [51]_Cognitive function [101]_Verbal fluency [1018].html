Category fluency was assessed by asking survey members to name as many different animals as possible in 1 minute. The score is the total number of animals named, allowing anything belonging to the animal kingdom (from amoeba to humans), but not counting repetitions, redundancies (e.g. brown cow, spotted cow) or proper names (e.g. Rover, Kitty).
