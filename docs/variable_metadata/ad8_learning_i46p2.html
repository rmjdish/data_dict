Notes Insight46 phase 2 AD8 - a short interview administered to a participant's informant to distinguish those with very mild dementia from those without dementia. A response of ?Yes, a change? indicates that there has been a change in the last several years caused by cognitive (thinking and memory) problems. An AD8 total score (ad8_totrec_i46p1) greater than or equal to 2 indicates that cognitive impairment is likely to be present. Also collected at phase 1 - see library file I46_Memory.
