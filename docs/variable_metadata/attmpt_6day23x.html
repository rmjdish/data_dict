Six day word recall [1024]
During the home visit, the participant were shown a list of words and was asked several times to write down all the words that they could remember. After 6 days, participant is asked: When I tell you to start, I would like you to write down all the words you can remember now on the lines provided. I will give you one minute to do this ? I will tell you when to start and stop.
