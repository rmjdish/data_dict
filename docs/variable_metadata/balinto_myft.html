Category Memberships

Standing balance [8083]
Study participants were asked by the attending nurse whether they would be willing to perform tests of standing balance. If willing and able (reasons for inability included health reasons and equipment not available) two tests of standing balance were performed, one with eyes open and one with eyes closed. Participants who were unable to do the test during the 2023 Home visit were asked to when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
