Reason Sensitive Contains age information.
Notes Insight46 phase 1 brain-predicted age was produced for all participants with a T1 MRI scan which passed quality control. The bl_bpa_adjusted variable is the brain-predicted age after adjustment for age bias. The age bias adjustment was performed by subtracting 3.33 from the unadjusted brain age and dividing by 0.91. These values were the intercept and slope of the model in the training dataset of brain-predicted age regressed on chronological age.
