Chair rises [1152]
During the clinic (or home) visit at age 60-64 years study participants were asked by the attending nurse whether they would be willing to be timed performing 10 chair rises. If willing and able (reasons for inability included health reasons and equipment not available) one test of 10 chair rises was performed.
