Category Memberships

Chair rises [8082]
Study participants were asked by the attending nurse whether they would be willing to be timed performing 10 chair rises. If willing and able (reasons for inability included health reasons and equipment not available) one test of 10 chair rises was performed. Participants who were unable to do the test during the 2023 Home visit were asked to when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
