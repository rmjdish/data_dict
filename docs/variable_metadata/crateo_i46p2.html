Notes Insight46 phase 2 circle-tracing and serial subtraction task. Participants were instructed to trace clockwise round the circle using a stylus as quickly and accurately as possible. In the direct condition, participants could see their hand and the path they were tracing on the tablet screen. In the indirect condition, the laptop was covered by an upturned box with the front open to allow the participant to put their hand inside.The task was administered in a single-task format (no subtraction) and a dual-task format with a concurrent task of serial subtraction. Also collected at phase 1 - see library file I46_neuropsychology
