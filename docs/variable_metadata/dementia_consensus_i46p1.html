Notes Insight46 phase 1 mild cognitive impairment/dementia. Dementia was determined at an adjudication meeting involving study doctors, psychologists and other team members who assessed the participant. It was based on self-reported history by the participant, informant history, clinician assessment and performance on cognitive testing. Also assessed at phase 2 - see library file I46P2_NeuroPsyc_Cognitive.
