General health questionnaire (GHQ) - screening for psychiatric disorder [8031]
The GHQ is a series of questions which act as a screening device for identifying minor psychiatric disorders in the general population. The version used by the NSHD is the GHQ-28: a 28 item scaled version which assesses somatic symptoms, anxiety and insomnia, social dysfunction and severe depression. Participants who were unable to answer the questions during the 2023 Home visit were asked to when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
