Hand grip strength [8081]
Study participants were asked by the attending nurse whether they would be willing to have their grip strength assessed. If willing and able (reasons for inability included health reasons and broken dynamometers) up to 6 tests of grip strength were performed, 3 in the dominant hand and 3 in the non-dominant hand, using a Nottingham electronic dynamometer. Participants who were unable to do the test during the 2023 Home visit were asked to when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
