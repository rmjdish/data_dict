Category Memberships

Spirometry [8071]
This category contains data on breath spirometry. The participant is asked to record two to three blows (lasting for at least 6 seconds) within a period of about 6 minutes. The computer compares the reproducibility of the first two blows and, if acceptable (defined as a <5% difference in forced volume vital capacity (FVC) & Forced Expiratory Volume in 1 second (FEV1)), will indicate that the third blow is not required. Participants who were unable to do the test during the 2023 Home visit were asked to when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
