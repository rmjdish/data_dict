This category contains the psychiatric symptom frequency inventory scale (PSF; Rodgers, 1994). This was administered by nurses. The PSF is an interview-based scale of symptoms of anxiety and depression over the previous 12 months. The reliability and validity of this measure is reported by Lindelow et al. (1997).
