Notes Insight46 phase 2 Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI): designed to assess sleep quality and sleep distubance within the last month. Eight domains are assessed including sleep quality, sleep latency, sleep duration, habitual sleep efficiency, sleep disturbance, use of sleep medication, and daytime dysfunction. A global score is derived as the sum of the domain score. A global PSQI>5 represents poor sleep quality. Not performed at phase 1.
