Notes Insight46 phase 2 Two related tasks ? Choice Reaction Time (RT) and Response Inhibition ? were combined into one experiment. The Choice RT task had 2 blocks of 12 trials each. The Response Inhibition task were 2 blocks of 48 trials each. For both tasks, participants were instructed to press the correct button on the response box (left or right) as quickly as possible, according to the stimulus on the screen. Also collected at phase 1 - see I46_neuropsychology.
