General health questionnaire (GHQ) - screening for psychiatric disorder [5203]
The GHQ is a series of questions which act as a screening device for identifying minor psychiatric disorders in the general population. The version used by the NSHD is the GHQ-28: a 28 item scaled version which assesses somatic symptoms, anxiety and insomnia, social dysfunction and severe depression.
