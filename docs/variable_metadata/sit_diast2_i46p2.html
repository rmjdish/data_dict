Notes Insight46 phase 2 blood pressure and pulse rate measures. Measurements were performed twice (one minute apart) in the left arm after 3 minutes of sitting down, and then twice (one minute apart) following 3 minutes in the recumbent position, and then twice (one minute apart) following 3 minutes of standing quietly. Lying and standing measures were also taken in phase 1 - see library file I46_bloodPressure.
