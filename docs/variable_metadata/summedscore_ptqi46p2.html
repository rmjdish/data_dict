Notes Insight46 phase 2 Perseverative Thinking Questionnaire (PTQ): assesses repetitive negative thinking; consists of 15 statements, and participants are asked to rate the extent to which each statement applies to them on a scale from 0 (never) to 4 (almost always). Scores are summed to give a total score which ranges from 0 to 60. The PTQ was introduced part-way through data collection for phase 2. It was not performed in phase 1.
