Notes Insight46 phase 2 CSF biomarker analysis - details of the commercially available Lumipulse AB40, AB42, p-tau181 and t-tau assays are given in the kit inserts and they were all used as per the manufacturer?s instructions. Measurements were undertaken in singlicate, using two reagent batches: the first 72 samples were assayed in 2019 for an interim analysis (1) and the remaining 62 samples were assayed in 2021. CSF was not collected at phase 1.
