Notes Insight46 phase 2 Epworth Sleepiness Scale - participants were asked to rate how likely they are to fall asleep in eight different situations, and the score from each situation was summed to give a total score out of 24. A total score of 16 or more indicates a high level of daytime sleepiness. Not administered at phase 1.
