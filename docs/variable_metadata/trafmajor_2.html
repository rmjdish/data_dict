Notes Land Use Regression (LUR) was used to estimate ambient air pollution concentrations at the home addresses of the NSHD participants. LUR combines the actual measurement of air pollution done between 26 Jan 2010 - 18 January 2011 and the development of stochastic models using predictor variables such as traffic variables, population density and meteorology derived with Geographical Information Systems (GIS) to develop statistical models using linear regression
