Memory (word list memory test) [8022]
For each of three trials survey members were shown a list of 15 words at a rate of two seconds each, then were asked to write down as many words recalled as possible. Participants who were unable to do the test during the 2023 Home visit were asked to do the test when they took part in the Myofit (2020-2025) sub-study.
